ABOUT US

Welcome to ALWAFA FOOD STUFF TRADING L.L.C Nestled in the heart of Dubai, A leading player in the import and export of premium fruits and vegetables for over 25 years. With a rich history of excellence and a deep understanding of the global market, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking high-quality produce.
Founded on the principles of quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, ALWAFA FOOD STUFF TRADING L.L.C has grown from a local trading company to a global enterprise with a diverse portfolio of products. Our team of experienced professionals has worked tirelessly to build a network of trusted suppliers and partners across the globe, ensuring that our customers receive only the freshest and best produce available.
Global Presence
"Experience the freshness of global markets with our international exporting and importing services for fruits and vegetables.
